## ✨ Features

- Supports **30+ currency pairs** including Majors, Crosses, Gold, and Indices
- Calculates **Lot Size, Risk Amount, Pips at Risk, and Position Size**
- Works on **desktop and mobile** — fully responsive
- **Enter key** support for fast calculations
- Clean animated results display
- Zero dependencies — no internet required after loading

## 🧮 How It Works

Enter your:
1. **Currency Pair** — e.g. EUR/USD, XAU/USD
2. **Entry Price** — where you plan to enter the trade
3. **Stop Loss Price** — where your stop loss is set
4. **Account Balance** — your total account in USD
5. **Risk %** — how much of your balance you want to risk

The calculator instantly tells you the correct **lot size** to use so you never over-risk a trade.
